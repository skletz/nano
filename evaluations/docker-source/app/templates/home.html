Welcome to Nano!

This docker image is used to demonstrate and reproduce all results of MMSYS 2019 paper: A Comparative Study of Video Annotation Tools for Scene Understanding: Yet (not) another Annotation Tool. The contributions made in this paper can be summarized as follows:

  • We summarize and review existing video annotation tools, their functionalities and provided features.
  • We conduct an experimental evaluation based on a user study with 45 subjects aiming at investigating the extent of annotation efficiency using different video annotation tools.
  • We analyze the impact of different video scenes, which cover varied annotation conditions, such as fast object or camera motion, camera panning and occlusion.
  • We provide evaluation input as well as output data together with all required source code for analyzing the user study http://doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.2553888.
